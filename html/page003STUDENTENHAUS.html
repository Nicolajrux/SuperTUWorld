Physics Building [EW]

Welcome to the EW building, the new home for all things Physics! The old hangout? That’s the ER building, but we’re all about the shiny new digs here. Don't forget to check out the Physics library right here if you're in the mood for some study time.

Thinking about diving into some physics courses? Swing by the student center for physics to get all the info you need over here.

Need a place to hit the books? The PC pool (EW019/20) is a rad spot for group study, loaded with workstations and Linux PCs. Prefer to work solo? The Säulenraum (EW016) has got you covered!

And let’s not forget about Atomic (EW021/22)—the ultimate chill zone for physics students! Grab a coffee, mate, Spezi, or Sterni, and kick back with your friends.

Oh, and if you need to get organized, the Ini room is run by your fellow physics students, who are busy organizing exam protocols and making sure your voice is heard in university committees.
