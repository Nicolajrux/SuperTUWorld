Main Cafeteria

When: Monday to Friday, 07:30 - 16:00. Get your coffee and snacks as early as 07:30, breakfast kicks off at 08:30, and the good stuff—hot meals—start rolling from 11:30 to 15:00.

Where: It's right between the [EW] building and UDK. You can't miss it!

What to bring: Don't forget your Mensa Card and student ID, or you'll be paying the full price (ouch). You can top up your card on the ground floor.

Next Magic Word: The next Magic Word is hidden in the bottom left of the picture in the cafeteria. It's 13 letters, ALL CAPS, no spaces or special characters.

Next stop: The Physics building [EW].

Other Food Options: The main cafeteria's cool, but you can check out other spots on campus here for their opening times. Wanna see what's on the menu? Check out the MENU. Heads up, outside opening hours, food on campus can be tricky to find. If you're still hungry, swing by Rüya or Tu Long next door for some good eats.

Pro Tip: If you're a fan of sparkling water, make a quick detour to the UDK building next door. Right after you walk in, on the right, there's a water fountain with sparkling water. I highly recommend it!
