Mario want to study math! You might have already noticed the little Mario character running around the campus. Solve every riddle and remember alls the magic words. For the final step, grab your PC or notebook. You can use the arrow keys to guide Mario to the spot where the Mathbuilding is on the map. If you manage to reach the correct location, a mystery box will appear, and you can enter the last magic word, which consts of the first letter of every password starting with the one of the Main Building. Placing Mario exactly on the right spot isn't so easy!
